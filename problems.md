# Problems Addressed

## 1. Resume Optimization Gap
Most candidates do not know how to tailor resumes for specific job descriptions.

## 2. ATS Rejection Issue
Resumes get rejected due to:
- Missing keywords
- Poor formatting
- Low semantic relevance

## 3. Lack of Career Guidance
Users lack clarity on:
- Suitable job roles
- Missing skills
- Improvement areas

## 4. No Feedback Loop
Users cannot track:
- Progress over time
- Resume improvements
- ATS score changes

## 5. Fragmented Tools
Existing solutions are scattered:
- Resume builders
- ATS checkers
- Career advice tools

## Solution
A unified **AI Career Copilot** that combines:
- Resume optimization
- ATS scoring
- Job recommendations
- Skill roadmap
- AI chat assistant
